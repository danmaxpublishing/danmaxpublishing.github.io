Roadmap

Where this is going

Honest categories only: shipped is verifiable in the changelog, next is committed, exploring is research — not a promise. Nothing here is a pre-order pitch.

Photo Mode Pro

Shipped

v1.0 – v1.2 · June 2026

The system

Controller, free camera, natural-unit adjustments, presets, supersampled capture, themed UI — then URP, HDRP and Built-in backends with the public capability system.

v1.3 · June 2026

Reach & rigor

Minimum Unity dropped to 2021.3 LTS, IL2CPP with high stripping verified, zero-per-frame-GC guarantee, EventSystem auto-creation, scene-change safety.

v1.4 · June 2026

Onboarding

Setup Wizard with one-click fixes, welcome window, pipeline-aware demo, self-test diagnostics, true log-stop aperture, docs site and full manual.

Submitted

Asset Store launch

v1.4.0 is submitted and in review. Get an email on launch day.

Next

Post-launch

Launch feedback round

The first post-launch update is reserved for whatever real users hit first — reviews, support email and GitHub issues set the priority list, and fixes ship with the same test gates as everything else.

Committed

Portrait & unusual aspect ratios

Hint chips can overlap on tall portrait canvases — a known v1.4 limitation. A layout rework for extreme aspect ratios is queued.

Exploring

Research areas — listed so you know what's on the bench, not to sell futures.

Research

WebGL capture

Capture-to-disk doesn't exist on WebGL; a browser-download capture path is being prototyped. (The rest of the photo mode already runs — this site's demo build is proof.)

Research

Mobile touch UI

The UI is gamepad- and cursor-first today. A touch layout with gesture camera controls is under evaluation.

Research

HDRP transparent capture

HDRP can't produce clean alpha without modifying the project's HDRP asset. Investigating a safe, opt-in path.

Beyond Photo Mode Pro

The next tool is in development

Unannounced until it survives the same gauntlet as Photo Mode Pro — full tests, full docs, honest store page. It gets announced here first.
